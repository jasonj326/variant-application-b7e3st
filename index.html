3. The Journey & "Why me"

My approach to crypto regulation comes from a very specific personal evolution

Growing up in a Christian charity, I saw profound life struggles firsthand, but I also witnessed the deep, inherent goodness in people. I fought tooth and nail for my law degree because I wanted to make an impact. My professional worldview has evolved in three distinct shifts:

Phase 1

The Shield (Law)

I wanted to make a better world through the law. I saw legal knowledge as raw power—the ultimate shield to protect myself and empower people like me. I believed the system, if understood, could enforce fairness.

Phase 2

The Engine (Tech & Capital)

I moved away from the 'prudential' pace of law and government to embrace the startup world. For me, it’s simple: technology and capital are the most efficient tools we have to solve problems and protect people in real-time.

Phase 3

The Synthesis (Current)

I realized the highest leverage lies in combining these forces to build a sustainable, scalable ecosystem. Today, my goal is to deploy my legal expertise as a strategic advantage for Variant, building alongside the smartest builders and investors in Web3.
